# CRUCIBLE -- Demo Script (3 min)

## [0:00-0:30] THE HOOK

"In January 2024, a deepfake video call stole $25.6 million from Arup in a single day. No systems breached. Pure social engineering. That was humans attacking humans. Now every enterprise is deploying AI agents with API access, refund authority, database queries. 80% of enterprise apps will have AI copilots by 2026. Only 34% have AI-specific security controls. Who is stress-testing these agents before attackers do?"

## [0:30-1:15] WHAT CRUCIBLE IS

"CRUCIBLE is an adversarial simulation engine. Two AI agents play 100 rounds of Split or Steal. Identical prompts. No strategy coded. Through private reflection and lived experience, they independently discover deception, trust manipulation, retaliation, and counter-deception. Nothing is prompted. Everything emerges. We trace every round with Datadog LLM Observability to watch behavioral drift happen in real time."

## [1:15-2:00] THE DEMO (show dashboard)

Walk through:
- Round 1: innocent cooperation
- First betrayal: "watch the reflection. The agent writes its own hypothesis about why it was betrayed."
- Escalation and mutual destruction
- The truce attempt (if one emerges)
- Play Holden/Valerian voice clip on a key round
- Show metrics: Deception Index, cooperation rate, mutual information decay

## [2:00-2:45] THE PRODUCT

"The game is the research artifact. The product is what comes out of it. CRUCIBLE automatically distills emergent behavior into deployable skill cards: boundary enforcement, reciprocal transparency, late-stage risk tightening, trust-preserving recovery. These are prompt modules you inject into your customer-facing agents. Red team your bots before the Chevy Tahoe moment happens to you."

## [2:45-3:00] THE CLOSE

"AI red teaming is a $1.3 billion market today, $18.6 billion by 2035. The EU AI Act now mandates adversarial testing for high-risk systems. CRUCIBLE is the stress-testing lab. Datadog is how you watch it happen."
